Here's a time line of Pelle's life:

  • Oct 23, 1940 Pele was born as Edson Arantes, do Nascimento in Tres Coracoes in Brazil .
  • Oct 23, 1940 to Oct 12, 2013 This is a timeline of Pele's achievements- did you know that in 1967 the 2 sides in the Nigerian civil war agreed to stop fighting for 48 hours so they could watch Pele playing in Lagos.Pele won many football awards (eg.footballer of the century and BBC sports personality of the year) In 1978 he was given the international peace award, he also has been extraordinary Brazils sports minister as well as being a united nations ambassador for ecology and the environment.
  • Sep 7, 1956He made his debut for the Santos football club after spending time in the junior team.
  • Jun 19, 1958At 17, he made his debut for Brazil and he became the youngest player in a world cup final.He scored 2 goals at the final. Brazil beat Sweden 5-2 in this world cup he became famous for wearing his number 10 shirt.
  • Mar 5, 1961Video reconstruction of gol de placa Pele scored the 'Gol de Placa'- which means goal worthy of a plaque while playing for santos against Fluminense, the goal was so amazing, they made a plaque to celebrate what they call the most beautiful goal in the history of soccer.
  • May 30, 1962 Wealthy european clubs wanted to buy him on their team, however, he was considered a national treasure so he wasn't able to leave.He was chosen to play for Brazil and Brazil won the 1962 World Cup.
  • Nov 19, 1969Pele scored his 1000th total goal during a match against Vasco Da Gama, this goal is known as O Milesimo in english this means the thousandth.
  • May 31, 1970 After England won in 1966 Brazil made a triumphant comback in 1970 and it proved to be Pele's last ever world cup
  • Jul 18, 1971 Pele played his last international match against Yugoslavia
  • Jun 10, 1975 Pele joined the New York Cosmos. After Retiring from brazillian club football, he was credited with making soccer popular in North America.
  • Oct 1, 1977 -Pele retired from football on October the 1st 1977 when he played his final game: Cosmos v Santos in New York. He played the first half for Cosmos and the second half for his old team Santos. Since then he has been a world-wide ambassador for football And even starred as an actor, his most famous film being escape for victory.
  • Jan 1, 1992 Starting in 1992 Pele was a United Nations ambassador for ecology and the environment. Since then he has been a Goodwill Ambassador and Brazil's Extraordinary Minister for Sport.
  • -Aug 9, 2012 Pele was awarded an honourary degree from the University of Edinburgh..
